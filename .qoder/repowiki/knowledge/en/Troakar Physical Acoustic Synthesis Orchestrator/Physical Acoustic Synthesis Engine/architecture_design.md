- **Core Simulation Layer**: `core_taichi.py` implements a GPU-accelerated Finite-Difference Time-Domain (FDTD) solver using Taichi kernels (`step_fdtd_anisotropic_ultimate`, `step_fdtd_heterogeneous`) to model wave propagation on 2D grids with support for anisotropic and heterogeneous materials.
- **Synthesis Strategies**: The module provides two distinct synthesis paths: `generate_fdtd_ir` for full physical grid simulation and `generate_physical_ir` in `core_dsp.py` for efficient modal cloud synthesis and additive modeling of resonant modes.
- **Tactile & Texture Sub-systems**: `tactile.py` and `shell_texture.py` generate micro-textural audio layers (fibrousness, granularity, fluidity) driven by physical telemetry (velocity, stress) from the core simulators, adding material-specific surface noise.
- **Geometry & Spatialization**: `geometry.py` handles instrument mask generation and strike/pickup point mapping, while `spatial.py` and `pyroomacoustics` integration in `core_taichi.py` apply distance-based air absorption and shoebox room reverberation.
- **Instrument Specialization**: `core_drums.py` extends the DSP pipeline with specific models for membrane tension, snare wire rattling, and cymbal bloom effects.
